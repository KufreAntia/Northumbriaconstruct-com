Northumbria Construct spotlighted at the 2026 Summer Congregation.

Northumbria Construct and the work led by founder Kufre Antia were featured at Northumbria University’s 2026 Annual Summer Congregation, bringing wider attention to the platform’s leadership and professional engagement.

The moment reflects a growing community connecting emerging and early- to mid-career professionals with industry, professional institutions and opportunities to learn, lead and progress.
