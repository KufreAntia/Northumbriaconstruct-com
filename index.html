Northumbria Construct spotlighted at the 2026 Summer Congregation.

We were featured at Northumbria University’s 2026 Annual Summer Congregation, bringing wider attention to our efforts to connect people with industry, professional institutions and meaningful opportunities to develop.

The feature marked an important moment for a growing community of emerging and early- to mid-career professionals learning, contributing and progressing together.

A milestone for our growing community and the work we continue to build.

Bridge the Gap.

Northumbria Construct brings emerging and established construction and project professionals together to connect, develop and progress.

Born from an award-winning entry in the Association for Project Management (APM) Project Management Challenge, we connect early- and mid-career professionals with universities, professional institutions and industry.
