Our purpose

From interest
to action.

We believe promising interests should have a clear route from ideation to action.

With the right support, networks and collaboration, ideas can develop into practical responses to real challenges in the built environment.

The Innovation Lab creates space for interests, ideas and project experience to develop into useful technologies, frameworks and solutions that improve how projects are planned, delivered and managed.

NorCon Projects

A Project
Operating System.

Not just another project management platform.

NorCon Projects is a developing project operating system focused on how projects are planned, governed, delivered, reviewed and improved.

Designed around project governance, structured collaboration and practical delivery, the platform aims to provide a lightweight yet powerful operating system that supports teams throughout the project lifecycle.

More information will be shared as development progresses.
